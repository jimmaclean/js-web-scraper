Product Information
The JBL Bar 2.0 All-in-One MK2 is a slim and unobtrusive soundbar, with the sonic impact that your TV’s been missing.

Unleash the sound potential
As TVs become ever sleeker, their built-in sound systems rarely live up to the super-sized images. Add a JBL Bar 2.0 All-in-One MK2 and you can bring back the sonic impact. Stereo, ‘racetrack’ speakers pair up with the 80 watt amp to deliver a clear, impactful sound – exactly what’s missing from your TV’s speakers.

Cinematic sound
Thanks to JBL’s Surround Sound technology, the sound isn’t just powerful, it’s fully immersive, too. With sound projected around the room, you get a cinematic feel, with the action greeting you for edge-of-seat tension and drama.

Also a Bluetooth music speaker
When you’d prefer music to movies, this versatile JBL soundbar steps up to the mark. Wireless, Bluetooth connectivity lets you link to your smartphone, tablet or other music source and stream from your favourite music service or podcast. Hear your favourite tracks from Spotify, Tidal, Apple Music or any other music app, with the benefit of JBL’s deep, powerful sound.

Easy to connect – including HDMI
Connecting to your TV is easy. Simply connect to the HDMI socket for a top quality, digital connection. You can connect other sources, too. The optical digital input is ideal for a games console, DVD player or even a compatible CD player.

Sleek design and wall mountable
Standing just 5.6cm tall, the Bar 2.0 MK2 fits neatly in front of your TV. Alternatively, use the supplied wall-mount bracket kit to position the soundbar just beneath your wall mounted TV.

Boost your TV’s sound for thrilling entertainment, with the JBL Bar 2.0 All-in-One MK2.
